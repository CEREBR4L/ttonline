About

What is tuesday temptation? Well let me tell you a little about what, who or why...

What?

A creative project or process... a thing I can take and do whatever I want to with it, without it having to represent me as a person or be tired with a face. A creative project just for creation, I have no outlined final goals just to keep making whatever I want and sharing it with whoever will come and look. I aim to work with new artist where I can and really just have the most fun I can with creativity be it photography, digital art, music or anything else...

Who?

Well... read the bit above, this doesn't matter, that is kind of the point.

Why?

To create the best I can with no strings attached, no one telling me what to do, it's a purely selfish project, persona, thing... In the end I just want to create, I don't want to sell you anything just share something with you, enjoy it if you can.

Thank you for reading!
